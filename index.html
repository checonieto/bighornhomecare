We understand that choosing care for your loved ones is a significant decision. Here is a comparison of the benefits of home care versus a nursing home:

Criteria Home Care Nursing Home
Cost Affordable, based on hours needed. High monthly fees.
Comfort Familiar home environment. Institutional setting.
Independence Promotes autonomy. Limited independence.
Personalized Attention One-on-one care. Shared caregiver resources.
Family Involvement Easy for family to visit. Scheduled visiting hours.
